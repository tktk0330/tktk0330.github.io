Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SIC BO Simulation ("the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • No personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.) is collected.
  • No location data is accessed.
  • No photos, contacts, or other device data is accessed.
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used.

2. Data Storage

All data generated during use of the App (simulation results, bet history, probability analysis) is stored locally on your device only and exists solely in memory during the app session. No data is saved to persistent storage or transmitted to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. As the App collects no personal information from any user, it complies with applicable children's privacy regulations.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
